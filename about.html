"Love Deaf. Involve Deaf." is a public education campaign initiated by the Singapore Association For The Deaf.

The Singapore Association For The Deaf believes in assisting the Deaf to achieve a better quality of life and to enable them to integrate to society. "Love Deaf. Involve Deaf." encourages you and I to look beyond one's disabilities and see persons with hearing disabilities as individuals, recognising them for their strenths, passion and abilities.

By nurturing the right attitudes when interacting with persons with hearing disabilities among us, let's us make our community a better place for those who are deaf!

Who are we

SADEAF

Singapore Association for the Deaf’s dedicated team of volunteers who assist and support the Deaf to reach their full potential.

OUR TEAM

Love Deaf. Involve Deaf.’s staffs collaborate with deaf individuals and associations all over Singapore to organize activities to encourage the inclusion of the Deaf in society.

YOU
